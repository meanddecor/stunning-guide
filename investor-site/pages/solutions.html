RevPAR Optimization Formula & Impact Calculation

Baseline Calculation:
• Property: 120 rooms, 62% occupancy, $165 ADR
• Current RevPAR = 0.62 × $165 = $102.30
• Annual rooms revenue = $102.30 × 365 × 120 = $4,481,580

Target Optimization:
• Improved occupancy: 64% (+2pp through better pricing/inventory)
• Improved ADR: $175 (+$10 through mix optimization)
• New RevPAR = 0.64 × $175 = $112.00
• New annual revenue = $112.00 × 365 × 120 = $4,905,600

Value Creation:
• Revenue increase = $424,020 annually
• Flow-through at 55% = $233,211 incremental GOP
• Client investment = $120K + 20% success fee = $166,642
• Client ROI = ($233,211 - $166,642) / $166,642 = 40% annually
