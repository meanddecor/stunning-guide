Compliance Framework

Export Company Compliance Scope

Export Controls

  • EAR classification for all products
  • License determination and applications
  • End-use and end-user verification
  • Technology transfer restrictions

Sanctions Screening

  • OFAC SDN list checking
  • Consolidated screening lists
  • Country embargo compliance
  • Beneficial ownership verification

Trade Documentation

  • Incoterms 2020 compliance
  • AES filing requirements
  • Certificate of origin accuracy
  • Customs valuation standards

Financial Controls

  • AML/KYC procedures
  • Wire transfer monitoring
  • Currency reporting (>$10K)
  • Tax compliance (1099s, W-8s)

Operational Controls

Approval Hierarchies

  • <$10K: Manager approval
  • $10-50K: Director approval
  • $50-100K: C-suite approval
  • >$100K: Board notification

Audit Schedule

  • Monthly: Transaction sampling
  • Quarterly: Compliance review
  • Annual: Full financial audit
  • Ad-hoc: Incident response

Training Requirements

  • Onboarding: 100% completion
  • Compliance: Quarterly updates
  • Security: Annual certification
  • Skills: Ongoing development

Contingency Planning

Scenario Response Plans

Scenario Immediate Actions Communication Plan Recovery Timeline
Major client loss Cost reduction, pipeline acceleration Team meeting within 24h, investor update 90 days to replace revenue
Data breach Isolate systems, engage forensics, notify counsel Client notification per regulations 48h containment, 30d remediation
Supply chain disruption Activate alternate suppliers, adjust pricing Client updates on delays/alternatives 2-4 weeks for new routing
Regulatory violation Stop activity, document facts, engage counsel Voluntary disclosure if applicable Varies by severity
