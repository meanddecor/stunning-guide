Risk & Compliance

Proactive risk management and regulatory compliance

Risk Assessment Matrix

Risk Likelihood Impact Mitigation Owner
Hotel uplift below target Medium Medium Rapid diagnostic pivot, fee at risk structure Revenue Lead
Export buyer default Low High Credit insurance, limits, LC requirements Finance
Freight rate spike Medium Medium Multi-forwarder bids, hedges, price buffers Operations
Sanctions breach Low High OFAC screening, written policy, quarterly audits Compliance
Supplier QC failure Medium Medium AQL inspections, blacklists, claims SOPs Operations

Compliance Framework

Export Compliance

  • EAR classification procedures
  • Incoterms alignment protocols
  • AES filing requirements
  • Sanctions screening (OFAC, UN, EU)
  • Restricted party database checks

Hotel Industry Compliance

  • Data privacy (GDPR, CCPA)
  • Franchise brand standards
  • Labor law compliance
  • PCI DSS for payment data
  • ADA accessibility requirements

Corporate Compliance

  • Anti-corruption policies
  • Whistleblower protections
  • Conflict of interest procedures
  • Document retention policies
  • Cybersecurity protocols

Risk Management Principles

"Compliance is non-negotiable"
"Screen, document, audit"
"Insurance as a last layer"
