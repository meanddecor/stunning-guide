Frequently Asked Questions

Direct answers to critical investor questions

What if the uplift does not appear in month one? +

We target leading indicators weekly and tie our fees to measured outcomes. Our sprint methodology includes weekly checkpoints where we can pivot tactics based on real-time data. Additionally, our fee structure includes performance components, aligning our compensation with client success.

How do you prevent export payment risk? +

We employ a multi-layered approach: credit insurance on all receivables, strict credit limits based on buyer history, optional letter of credit requirements for new relationships, and comprehensive documentation that meets insurance claim requirements. Our default rate target is under 0.5% of revenue.

Why choose you over established incumbents? +

Three key differentiators: First, we deliver operator-grade playbooks with measurable results, not theoretical frameworks. Second, our visible weekly cadence ensures rapid implementation versus quarterly check-ins. Third, our cash-cycle design and integrated approach reduces total cost of engagement by 40-60% versus traditional providers.

Can this scale without the founders? +

Yes. Our pod structure, comprehensive SOPs, and training academy create repeatable delivery independent of any individual. We're building institutional knowledge through documented playbooks, not personal relationships. By Month 12, we expect 80% of delivery to be handled by non-founder team members.

What is the failure line that triggers a pivot? +

We have clear red lines: customer churn exceeding 15% annually or CAC payback extending beyond 6 months triggers an immediate spending freeze and strategic review. These metrics are tracked weekly with automated alerts to the board.

How do you maintain quality during rapid scaling? +

Quality is maintained through three mechanisms: standardized SOPs that capture best practices, intensive 30-day onboarding for all client-facing staff, and weekly quality reviews with objective metrics. Each pod has a dedicated quality lead, and client NPS is tracked continuously with alerts for any score below 7.

What's your competitive moat? +

Our moat consists of: proprietary playbooks refined through hundreds of engagements, exclusive partnerships with key service providers, a compliance infrastructure that takes years to build, and most importantly, a track record of measurable results that compounds our credibility with each success.

How do you handle economic downturns? +

Our model is counter-cyclical in many ways. During downturns, hotels need cost optimization more than ever, making our services more valuable. Export buyers seek reliable, cost-effective suppliers. Our variable cost structure allows us to scale down if needed while maintaining core capabilities.

What are the key technology dependencies? +

We rely on proven, enterprise-grade systems: Salesforce for CRM, established BI platforms for analytics, and API integrations with major PMS/RMS systems. We maintain redundancy and avoid bleeding-edge technologies. All critical systems have SLAs and backup procedures.

How do you ensure regulatory compliance across markets? +

Compliance is built into our operating model with dedicated compliance officers, quarterly third-party audits, automated screening systems, and partnerships with local legal counsel in each market. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for violations and invest 3% of revenue in compliance infrastructure.
