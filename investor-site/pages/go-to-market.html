Go-to-Market Plan

Targeted approach to rapid market penetration

Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Hotel Consulting ICP

  • Type: Independent and lifestyle hotels
  • Size: 80-250 keys
  • RevPAR: $80-$200 range
  • Leadership: Owner-led or professional management
  • Pain: Margin pressure, outdated systems
  • Budget: $100k-$500k for improvements

Export Company ICP

  • Type: Mid-market importers/distributors
  • Volume: $5M-$50M annual imports
  • Categories: Food, consumer goods, materials
  • Geography: North America, Europe
  • Pain: Supply reliability, working capital
  • Credit: BB or better rating
