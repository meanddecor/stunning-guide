Months 0-3

Objectives

Critical Path Dependencies

Technology Foundation

  • CRM deployment → Sales efficiency
  • Analytics platform → Performance tracking
  • Compliance tools → Risk management
  • Integration APIs → Operational scale

Team Building

  • Pod 1 success → Pod 2 launch
  • Training program → Quality consistency
  • Leadership depth → Geographic expansion
  • Culture codification → Sustainable growth

Market Validation

  • Pilot results → Pricing confidence
  • Case studies → Sales acceleration
  • Client feedback → Product refinement
  • Partner traction → Channel leverage

Execution Risk Mitigation

Risk Mitigation Strategy Early Warning Signs
Talent acquisition delays Build pipeline early, use contractors, competitive comp Open roles >60 days
Pilot results below target Rapid iteration, close client collaboration, pivot ready Week 2 metrics off track
Technology implementation issues Phased rollout, vendor SLAs, internal capabilities Integration delays >2 weeks
Market adoption slower than expected Multiple channels, value proof points, pricing flexibility Pipeline conversion <20%
Working capital constraints Credit facility in place, milestone-based funding, tight AR DSO trending >75 days
